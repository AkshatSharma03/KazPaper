QUESTIONS ANSWERED BY THIS DOCUMENT:

Q: "Who exports what to whom?"
A: Kazakhstan exports raw materials (metals, energy, chemicals) TO China.
   Kazakhstan imports manufactured goods (machinery, vehicles, textiles) 
   FROM China. All from Kazakhstan's perspective.

Q: "Why is this a deficit?"
A: Because Kazakhstan imports 3.8x MORE value FROM China than 
   it exports TO China. ($31.9B imports vs $29.7B exports post-2023)

Q: "Is China dependent on Kazakhstan?"
A: Not shown in this document (we focus on Kazakhstan's perspective).
   But Kazakhstan is VERY dependent on China for manufactured goods.

Q: "What changed in 2023?"
A: Suddenly Kazakhstan's imports from China nearly tripled while 
   exports barely doubled. This flipped the trade balance from 
   surplus to deficit.
