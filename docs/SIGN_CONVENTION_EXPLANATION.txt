DEFICIT RATIO SIGN CONVENTION
=============================================================

Formula: deficit_ratio = (imports - exports) / (imports + exports)

INTERPRETATION:
  - Negative values: Kazakhstan TRADE SURPLUS (exports > imports)
  - Zero: Balanced trade (exports = imports)
  - Positive values: Kazakhstan TRADE DEFICIT (imports > exports)

EXAMPLES FROM DATA:
  2015: -0.037  (small surplus, exports > imports)
  2020: -0.555  (large surplus, imports collapsed)
  2023: +0.064  (deficit, imports > exports)

KEY TURNING POINT:
  2023 was the first year with a positive deficit ratio,
  indicating Kazakhstan's first trade deficit with China
  in the 2015-2024 period.

GRAPHICAL CONVENTION:
  In trade balance figures:
  - Bars above zero = Kazakhstan surplus
  - Bars below zero = Kazakhstan deficit
  
  In deficit ratio figures:
  - Negative values (below zero line) = surplus years
  - Positive values (above zero line) = deficit years

IMPORTANT FOR PAPER:
  Include one explicit sentence in the manuscript:
  "The deficit ratio is negative when Kazakhstan runs a 
  trade surplus (exports exceed imports) and turns positive 
  when imports exceed exports, with 2023 marking the first 
  deficit year in the series."
